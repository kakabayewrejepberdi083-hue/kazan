Kazan: The Third Capital of RussiaKazan is the capital and largest city of the Republic of Tataristan, Russia. Known as the "Third Capital of Russia," it is a vibrant city where Europe and Asia meet. The city is famous for its rich history, unique culture, and the Kazan Kremlin, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Kazan is a place where diverse religions and ethnicities live together in harmony, showcased by the beautiful Kul Sharif Mosque standing near the Orthodox Cathedral.
